Sentiment Manager

These weights tune interpretation preferences only. They do not modify signal engine code.

Overall Sentiment

What it affects: Composite sentiment interpretation across all streams.

How it changes interpretation: Adjusts aggregate sentiment emphasis against technical context.

Impact on confidence: Higher influence increases confidence sensitivity to sentiment shifts.

Institutional Sentiment

What it affects: Influences long-term trend durability and continuation probability.

How it changes interpretation: Raises or lowers trust in trend signals when institutional flow aligns.

Impact on confidence: High influence can materially shift conviction in longer-horizon setups.

News Sentiment

What it affects: Event-driven interpretation around headlines and macro narrative.

How it changes interpretation: Makes the system more or less reactive to fresh news flow.

Impact on confidence: Higher influence amplifies confidence adjustments during major events.

Social Sentiment

What it affects: Near-term crowd positioning and participation pressure.

How it changes interpretation: Biases interpretation toward short-term sentiment momentum.

Impact on confidence: High influence increases confidence volatility in fast-moving names.

Defines behavioural trading style. Adjusts interpretation and risk appetite without modifying core signal logic.

Help

  • Sentiment streams represent interpretive weighting, not direct changes to compute engine internals.
  • Create a tactic by selecting a preset and adding natural-language instructions, then generate and review the diff table.
  • Activation stores the tactic profile and marks it as active in admin state.
  • This does not change `compute_basic_signal` or `compute_trade_signal` execution logic.
